Wi-Fi Hot Spots in Downtown Brantford

February 16th,2017

One of the many benefits of spending time in downtown Brantford is the abundance of businesses offering free Wi-Fi connections. Next time you need to study, browse social media, or get some work done on the go, visit one of the following locations in Downtown Brantford

Bar Burrito – Fresh Mexican Grill

Tex-Mex fare is on the menu at Bar Burrito. This popular restaurant offers gourmet menu items in a quick service setting that boasts great value and service.

Boston Pizza

Catch a game on the sports bar’s big screen TV’s or hang out with friends on the dining side. Boston Pizza offers a casual setting with over 100 menu items including gourmet pizzas made with their signature hand pressed dough

Coffee Culture Café & Eatery

Grab a cup of coffee made from high quality, whole roasted coffee beans and enjoy fresh sandwiches, soups and desserts. Coffee Culture provides a relaxing atmosphere reminiscent of a European café

Devlin’s Advocate

Share decadent appetizers and drinks in the elegant ambience at Devlin’s Advocate. Their varied high end menu has something that is sure to satisfy every taste and preference

The Kitchen

The Kitchen proudly offers fresh food made fast. Their many delicious options are all made with healthy ingredients that are locally sourced

Starving Artist Gallery Café

Enjoy art and entertainment while dining on crepes, panini’s, salads and homemade soups. The Starving Artist Gallery Café offers food that is fresh, never frozen, fried or microwaved.

Our Place Lounge and Eatery

Take a break and play darts, pool and ping pong at Our Place Lounge and Eatery. Enjoy a cold drink and chow down on Brick Oven Pizza & Panzerottis.

Pita Plus

Family owned and operated, Pita Plus serves Middle Eastern Cuisine and a variety of different wraps, plates and Shawarma at very reasonable price.

Rockling's Tap and Grill

Check out the daily specials at Rockling’s Tap and Grill and enjoy high quality, freshly prepared food at reasonable prices. With 14 beers on tap, there’s something for everyone at Rockling’s.

Tim Hortons

Their always fresh coffee, hot and cold specialty beverages and many delicious quick meal options will hit the spot any time of day. Tim Hortons is a great place to study, work or just hang out. Treat

Williams Fresh Café

Williams is a warm and welcoming place to enjoy great food, meet with friends, and a relaxing place for students gather. At Williams you will find fresh food, great coffee and friendly service every time
